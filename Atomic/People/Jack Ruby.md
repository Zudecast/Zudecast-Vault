## History
### [[Louis Jolyon West|Jolly West]] & Jack Ruby
 > (1963)
 
 > Following the murder of [[Lee Harvey Oswald]] in Dallas, Texas in 1963, Jack Ruby was held in an isolation cell in police custody. [[Louis Jolyon West|Jolly West]] was appointed as Ruby's psychiatrist, and pronounced him psychotic and delusional, and suggested further interrogation under the influence of sodium thiopental and hypnosis.
